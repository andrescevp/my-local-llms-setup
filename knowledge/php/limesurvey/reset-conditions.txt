In the case in which you wish to reset all the conditions that were created via our [[Setting conditions|conditions manager]], use the '''reset conditions''' function. 

To access it, select one of the survey-related settings and click on '''Tools''' located on the top toolbar:


<center>[[File:Reset conditions location.png]]</center>


Once clicked, you will have to confirm one last time that you wish to delete all the conditions before proceeding further:


<center>[[File:Reset conditions final confirmation.png]]</center>
